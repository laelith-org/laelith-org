## How to Contribute

If you are an author or artist who has contributed to Laelith and wish to make your work available under CC-BY 4.0 or a similar open license:

1. Contact the Laelith Foundation
2. Provide clear identification of the work(s) you wish to release
3. Confirm the license you wish to use (CC-BY 4.0 recommended)
4. Your work will be added to this page with proper attribution

## Attribution

When using content from this page, please provide proper attribution to the original authors/artists as listed, and include a link to this page or to the specific work's license.
